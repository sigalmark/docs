Getting Started

Set up your account and start issuing certificates in minutes.

1. Create Your Account

  1. Visit the Sigalmark platform and click Register.
  2. Enter your email address, choose a password, and optionally add your name.
  3. Check your inbox for a verification email and click the link to confirm your account.
Note You must verify your email address before you can log in and access the platform.

2. Join an Organisation

Sigalmark is multi-tenant — each organisation operates in its own workspace. To join an organisation, you need an invitation from an Admin of that organisation.

  1. Ask your organisation's Admin to send you an invitation via the Tenant > Roles & Invitations page.
  2. You will receive an email with a link to accept the invitation.
  3. Click the link, log in (or register if you haven't yet), and you will be added to the organisation.

3. Log In

  1. Go to the login page and enter your email and password.
  2. If your account has two-factor authentication (2FA) enabled, enter the 6-digit code from your authenticator app.
  3. You will be directed to the Certificates page.

4. Explore the Platform

Once logged in, you will see the main areas of the platform:

  • Certificates — Issue, manage, and track certificates. This is the main hub.
  • Tenant Settings — Manage your organisation's branding, members, subscription, and billing (Admin only).
  • Profile — Update your personal details and enable 2FA.
  • Verify — Public certificate verification page (no login required).

5. Enable Two-Factor Authentication

For added security, we recommend enabling 2FA on your account. Go to your Profile page and follow the setup wizard to link an authenticator app (such as Google Authenticator or Authy).

6. Organisation Settings (Admin)

If you are an Admin, you can configure your organisation under Tenant Settings:

  • Branding — Upload your logo and customise QR code styling (colours, dot styles, templates).
  • Members — Invite team members and assign roles (Member or Admin).
  • Subscription — View your plan, seat and certificate limits, and purchase add-ons.
  • Billing — Manage payment methods and view invoices via the Stripe portal.
