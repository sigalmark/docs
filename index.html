What is Sigalmark?

Sigalmark is a multi-tenant, blockchain-based digital certificate platform. Organisations use Sigalmark to issue tamper-proof certificates as ERC-721 NFTs on the blockchain. Each certificate includes a branded QR code that anyone can scan to instantly verify its authenticity.

Key Features

  • Blockchain-backed certificates — Certificates are minted as NFTs, making them tamper-proof and independently verifiable.
  • Branded QR codes — Each certificate includes a custom QR code with your organisation's branding.
  • Multi-tenant platform — Each organisation gets its own workspace with member management, branding, and billing.
  • Batch issuance — Issue up to 25 certificates at a time via CSV upload.
  • Email delivery — Send certificates directly to recipients with a single click.
  • Mobile verification — Scan and verify certificates on the go with the Sigalmark mobile app.
  • Expiry notifications — Automated email reminders before and after certificate expiry.
