Certificate Verification

Verify the authenticity of any Sigalmark certificate.

Overview

Every Sigalmark certificate is minted as an NFT on the blockchain. This means anyone can independently verify that a certificate is authentic, has not been tampered with, and is still valid — without needing to contact the issuing organisation.

Verification Methods

There are several ways to verify a certificate:

1. Scan the QR Code (Mobile App)

The fastest way to verify a certificate:

  1. Open the Sigalmark mobile app on your phone.
  2. Tap Start Scanning.
  3. Point your camera at the QR code on the certificate.
  4. The app will automatically read the QR code and verify the certificate on the blockchain.
  5. You will see a pass or fail result with the certificate details.
Tip You can also scan Sigalmark QR codes with your phone's built-in camera app. The QR code contains a short URL that opens the web verification page.

2. Scan or Upload a QR Code (Web)

  1. Go to the Verify page on the Sigalmark website.
  2. Either use the QR Scanner button to scan with your camera, or drag-and-drop / upload a QR code image.
  3. The system will decode the QR code and verify the certificate automatically.

3. Paste a URL

  1. Go to the Verify page.
  2. Paste a certificate short URL (e.g. sm1.it/AbCd) or full verification URL into the input field.
  3. Click Verify.

4. Manual Lookup

If you have the technical details of a certificate:

  1. Go to the Verify page.
  2. Enter the Contract Address, Token ID, and Network ID.
  3. Click Verify.

Verification Results

After verification, you will see the certificate details:

  • Status — Valid, Expired, or Revoked.
  • Name — The certified company or individual.
  • Competency — The qualification or certification type.
  • Issued — The date the certificate was issued.
  • Expiry — The date the certificate expires.
  • Network — The blockchain network where the certificate is stored.

Status Meanings

StatusWhat It Means
ValidThe certificate is authentic and has not expired or been revoked.
ExpiredThe certificate has passed its expiry date. It was authentic but is no longer current.
RevokedThe certificate has been permanently revoked by the issuing organisation.

Branded Verification Pages

Each organisation has a branded verification page at its own URL (e.g. sigalmark.com/your-org/contract-address). When someone scans a QR code or follows a certificate link, they see the verification result on a page branded with the issuing organisation's logo and colours.

Mobile App

The Sigalmark mobile app (available for iOS and Android) provides camera-based QR scanning optimised for certificate verification. Features include:

  • Real-time camera scanning with auto-verification.
  • Focus and zoom controls for scanning printed certificates.
  • Pass/fail result with full certificate details.
  • Works offline for scanning — blockchain verification requires an internet connection.
