Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about the Sigalmark platform.

General

What is Sigalmark?

Sigalmark is a blockchain-based digital certificate platform. Organisations use it to issue tamper-proof certificates as ERC-721 NFTs, which can be verified by anyone via a QR code scan or online lookup.

What does "blockchain-backed" mean for my certificates?

Each certificate is minted as an NFT (non-fungible token) on a blockchain network. This means the certificate data is stored on a decentralised, immutable ledger — it cannot be altered, forged, or deleted. Anyone can independently verify its authenticity.

Do I need cryptocurrency to use Sigalmark?

No. The platform handles all blockchain transactions behind the scenes. You do not need a crypto wallet or any cryptocurrency to issue or verify certificates.

How do I join an organisation?

You need an invitation from an Admin of the organisation. They can send you one from the Tenant > Roles & Invitations page. You will receive an email with a link to accept.

Certificates

How many certificates can I issue at once?

You can issue up to 25 certificates per batch via CSV upload. If your CSV has more than 25 rows, the platform will offer to split it into multiple batches automatically.

Can I revoke a certificate after issuing it?

Yes. You can revoke a certificate from the Issued tab. Revocation is permanent and updates the certificate's on-chain status. Revoked certificates will show as "Revoked" when someone verifies them.

Can I extend an expired certificate?

Yes. You can extend the expiry date of a certificate from the Issued tab, even after it has expired.

What happens when a certificate expires?

Expired certificates remain on the blockchain but show an "Expired" status when verified. The platform sends automated email notifications to certificate holders at 30 days, 7 days, and 1 day before expiry, and 7 days after expiry.

Batch Upload

What date format should I use in the CSV?

The expiry column accepts two formats:

  • ISO (recommended): YYYY-MM-DD (e.g. 2026-12-31)
  • US: MM/DD/YYYY (e.g. 12/31/2026)

We recommend ISO format to avoid ambiguity between day and month.

What is the imageURI column for?

The optional imageURI column lets you attach an image to each certificate. The value must be a direct URL to an image file (e.g. ending in .png or .jpg).

Important: Do not use webpage links (e.g. Google Drive sharing links or pages with embedded images). The URL must point directly to the image file so it can be downloaded and embedded in the certificate.

My CSV has more than 25 rows. What should I do?

Simply upload it as-is. The platform will detect the large file and offer to automatically split it into batches of 25 rows each, which are then minted sequentially.

What headers are required in the CSV?

Required: name, competency, expiry.
Optional: companyEmail, imageURI.

The header names are case-sensitive and must appear in the first row.

Verification

How do I verify a certificate?

The simplest way is to scan the QR code on the certificate with your phone camera or the Sigalmark mobile app. You can also go to the Verify page on the website and paste a URL, upload a QR code image, or enter the certificate's contract address and token ID manually.

Do I need an account to verify a certificate?

No. Certificate verification is public — anyone can verify a certificate without logging in.

What does "Certificate validated" mean?

When you see "Certificate validated" on the verification page, it means the system has successfully read the certificate data from the blockchain and confirmed it is a legitimate Sigalmark certificate. Check the status field (Valid, Expired, or Revoked) for the certificate's current standing.

Account & Security

How do I enable two-factor authentication?

Go to your Profile page and follow the 2FA setup wizard. You will need an authenticator app such as Google Authenticator or Authy.

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

Click Forgot Password on the login page. Enter your email address and you will receive a password reset link.
